Channels

  • Position
    • The position of each point represents the different combination of launch angle (degrees) and exit velocity (mph).
    • This is a good mapping, because it displays two very useful pieces of information in a very simple fashion. It does not do too much and allows the other components of the visualization to help the viewer better understand what is going on.
  • Vertical height
    • Represents the direction that baseballs are hit given the different launch angles they are hit at
    • This is a really good mapping becuase it does not overcrowd the graph but gives viewers a better understanding on how launch angle affects the way a baseball is hit. In this situation the channel is following the effectiveness principle well because it overpowers the launch angle attribute which can be confusing to people who don't have prior knowledge and overshadows it with a simplier explanation of what different launch angles result in. That is the easier and more effective takeaway between the the launch angle y-axis and the lines on top of it.
  • Color
    • Represents the scoring value of different points or different combinations of launch angle and exit velocity
    • This channel helps tie the visualization together and understand which combinations of x and y values produce the best results. The luminance is also very low on these colors which prevents the color from being too overpowering and take the attention away from the rest of the visualization

Colormap(s)

  • colormap 1
    • Represents the different scoring values of different combinations of exit velocity and launch angle.
    • This is a good mapping because it is using a divergent color map to represent the data. There is purple and orange representing the extreme lows and the extreme highs and then white in the middle to represent the neutral values. Becuase the respective colors get darker as they reach more extreme values, those values are given a higher visual emphasis which is perfect for what this visualization is trying to represent.
